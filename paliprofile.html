Treatment Journey

The path from diagnosis to recovery

Initial Diagnosis

August 2019

After experiencing persistent fatigue, weight loss, and abdominal discomfort, Prof. Paliwal sought medical attention. Comprehensive diagnostic tests, including imaging studies and liver biopsies, confirmed the presence of advanced liver cancer. His conventional healthcare providers offered a guarded prognosis with limited treatment options.

Beginning Homeopathic Treatment

October 2019

Seeking additional options, Prof. Paliwal consulted with Dr. Tomar. After a comprehensive assessment that considered his specific cancer type, overall health status, and constitutional factors, Dr. Tomar developed a personalized homeopathic treatment protocol. This included carefully selected remedies aimed at supporting liver function, addressing cancer-specific symptoms, and enhancing overall vitality.

First Significant Improvements

January-March 2020

Within 3-4 months of starting treatment, Prof. Paliwal began experiencing noticeable improvements in his energy levels and appetite. Laboratory tests showed a gradual decline in tumor markers and improved liver function parameters. These early positive indicators provided encouragement to continue with the homeopathic protocol.

Major Clinical Response

April-September 2020

Follow-up imaging and laboratory assessments during this period revealed a significant reduction in tumor size and a 65% decrease in tumor marker levels. Liver function tests continued to show improvement, and Prof. Paliwal was able to resume many of his normal daily activities with renewed energy and minimal discomfort.

Ongoing Treatment & Monitoring

October 2020 - Present

Prof. Paliwal continues with a maintenance homeopathic protocol, with regular follow-up consultations to monitor his condition and adjust treatment as needed. His case has remained stable for over two years, with no evidence of disease progression - an outcome that surprised his original medical team.
